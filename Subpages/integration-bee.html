Final Showdown

The final showdown follows the same rules of the MIT Integration Bee contest, and uses questions from their homepage.

The Rules:
  • Five questions in five minutes for each round
  • No +c, no simplification required. Answer must be boxed or circled
  • The best of three rounds wins

The contestants are clearly taking this matter very seriously.
